A Little About Ourselves

By Mae’s Design is an interior decorating business that focuses on modern, boho chic, and western themed home decorating. However, we also gather different crafts and DIY (do it yourself) projects wihtin our website to help you do some home decorating of you own. Mae Smith, By Mae's Design founder and head decorator, believed that interior decorating is what makes a house a home.

Whether you decide to use some of our tips and ideas to decorate or you hire us to complete an entire makeover, we are glad to help! If you have any questions about what other services we offer or would like to book a consultation appointment, please visit our Contact Page with all of our contact information.


Our Founding

By Mae's Design was established in 2000 by Mae Smith, who is the owner and head designer. In fact, By Mae’s Design has celebrated its twentieth anniversary this year! In 1996, Mae gained her cirtificate in Interior Decorating and, shortly after, gained work as a freelancer.

After she attained some work experience, Mae decided to start her own business doing what she loved. With the help of her sister, who studied business in college and recently graduated, they became business partners. Within the next two years, they rented an office building and advertised their new service. Starting in the small town of Tehachapi, where the two grew up, they have, in the past four years, spead out to two other neighboring cities. Now, they hope to extend their business to many more cities and create a second office location in Landcaster, CA.
